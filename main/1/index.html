Cottage Mashed Potatoes

4 cups warm smashed potatoes(without milk and butter)| 2 cups (16 ounces) small~curd cottage cheese| 3/4 cup sour cream| 1 to 2 tablespoons grated onion| 1~1/2 to 2 tablespoons salt| 1/2 teaspoon pepper| 2 tablespoons butter or margarine, melted| 1/4 cup sliced almonds, toasted|

1. mash your potatoes
2. add cottage cheese, sour cream, onion, salt and pepper put into a grease 2~qt baking dish
3. after that Drizzle with butter
4. set it into the oven at 350 for 30 Minutes
5. sprinkle with almonds
6. after that set it into the oven for 5 minutes long

then take it out and eat :D
