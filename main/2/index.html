Apple Pear Cake

ingredents that you need

2 cups shredded peeled apples
2 cups shredded peeled pears
2 cups sugar
1-1/4 cups vegetable oil
1 cup raisins
1 cup chopped pecans
2 eggs, beaten
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
3 cups all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking soda
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon salt


for Frosting

1 package (3 ounces) cream cheese,
softened
1/4 cup butter or margarine, softened
3 cups confectioners' sugar
2 tablespoons milk
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract


To Make "The Pear Cake"

1. Get a bowl
2. Add the first ingredients
3. Stir
4. Pour into a greased 13~in x 9~in x 2~in baking pan
5. Bake at 325 for one hour or untel a toothpick inserted near in the center cool it download



Frosting

1.put cream cheese and butter in a bowl
2.mix until fluffy
3.add sugar, milk and vanilla
4.mix well
5.pour/put in refrigerator
